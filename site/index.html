Why hire a mental-health-only agency instead of a general one?

Because the failure modes are different in this field. A general agency can make a pretty site; it usually can't tell you which intake-form setup leaks PHI, why therapy-seekers bounce off stock imagery, or how condition-plus-city searches actually fill caseloads. Specialization means we've already made the expensive mistakes — elsewhere, on our own time.

What actually works for attracting new therapy clients online?

The reliable stack, in order: a fast website that answers fees/insurance/approach clearly, local SEO so you appear where people search, a strong Google Business Profile and directory presence, and — once those convert — Google Ads for immediate volume. Social media supports trust; it rarely drives volume alone. We sequence them so each layer feeds the next.

What services does Willowdesk deliver?

Six: HIPAA-compliant website design and development, mobile app development, SEO and content marketing, Google Ads management, social media optimization, and AI marketing automation. Most practices start with the website + SEO pair; everything else layers on when the data says it should.

How long until SEO results show up?

Honest answer: local map-pack and long-tail improvements typically appear within 60–90 days; competitive city-wide rankings take 4–9 months of consistent work. Anyone promising page one in two weeks is selling you something else. Google Ads fills the gap while organic compounds.

Is everything you build HIPAA-compliant?

Every build is HIPAA-aware by design: BAA-covered form, booking and hosting providers wherever PHI flows, encryption in transit and at rest, and no PHI in analytics or ad pixels. We document these decisions per project. UK and Canadian practices get the equivalent UK-GDPR and PIPEDA treatment.

Do you work with solo practitioners, or only group practices?

Both. Solo therapists, group practices, psychiatry clinics and treatment centers all have plans scaled to their size — a solo practice doesn't need (or pay for) a treatment-center build. The free audit tells you which tier actually fits.
