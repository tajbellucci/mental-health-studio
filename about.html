The studio that only does mental health.

Mental Health Studio exists because clinicians kept telling the same story: the agency didn't get it. The tech worked, the compliance didn't. The site was pretty, the clients never came. We built the alternative — registered in Toronto, Ontario, with a founding team spanning Canada and Pakistan.

The origin

Born inside digital mental health, not advertising.

Our founder spent years building mental-health platforms — a national CBT platform, clinician tools, published digital-health research — and kept meeting excellent clinicians who were invisible online. Not for lack of skill. For lack of a partner who understood both the therapy room and the technology.
