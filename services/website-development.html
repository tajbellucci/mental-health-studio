FAQ

Website Design & Development questions.

How long does a website build take?

Typically two weeks from kickoff to launch for a solo practice; group practices and treatment centers with program pages run three to five weeks. You get a written timeline in the strategy stage.

Do I own the website?

Yes — domain, content, design and data are yours. No lock-in, and clean handover if you ever leave.

Can you migrate my existing site?

Yes, including moves off Squarespace and Wix with rankings preserved — redirects mapped one-to-one before anything switches.
