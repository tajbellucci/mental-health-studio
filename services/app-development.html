FAQ

App Development questions.

Does my practice actually need an app?

Often no — and we'll say so. Apps earn their cost for group practices and treatment centers with recurring programs, homework and cohorts. Solo practices usually get more from a great website plus booking.

How is client data protected?

Encryption in transit and at rest, BAA-covered infrastructure, no third-party analytics with PHI, and role-based access. You get the architecture documented.

What does an app cost?

Scoped case by case. A booking-and-reminders app is weeks of work; custom teletherapy and program features take longer. Fixed quote after the audit stage.
